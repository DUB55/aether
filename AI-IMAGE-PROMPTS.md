# AI Image Generator Prompts for Aether Interfaces

## Color Palette & Styling Guide
**Primary Colors:**
- Background: Deep Navy (#02040a, #0a0f1a)
- Primary: Cyan (#06b6d4, #22d3ee, #67e8f9)
- Text: Light Gray/Slate (#f8fafc, #e2e8f0, #94a3b8)
- Borders: Subtle White/Gray (rgba(255,255,255,0.06), rgba(255,255,255,0.12))

**Design Style:**
- Modern, minimalist interface design
- Glass morphism effects with subtle blur
- Rounded corners (12-24px radius)
- Gradient accents (cyan to blue)
- Dark theme with high contrast
- Clean typography with Inter font
- Subtle shadows and depth
- Professional, tech-forward aesthetic
